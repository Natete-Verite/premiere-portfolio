About me

Software Developer

Verite is an entry-level Software Developer from Rwanda who aspires to leave the world a better place than she found it. On her quest to achieving that, she embarked on a journey to creating and contributing to solving world problems using technology. Over the last couple of months, she developed her skills in Frontend Web development, Frontend Mobile Development, Backend Development, User Research and UI/UX Design. Honesty, diversity and empathy are core values she holds dear to her heart. During her free time, she enjoys spending time with children and doing community work volunteering.
